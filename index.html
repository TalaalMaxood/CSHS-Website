Computer Science Honor Society

As stated in the Virginia Computer Science Honor Society Constitution, the Deep Run Computer Science Honor Society aims to create enthusiasm for computer science and promote and recognize academic excellence the Computing Science disciplines in the students of secondary schools. Through service projects and active tutoring, we hope to better our school and community.

Join Us

Join CSHS

The Deep Run Chapter of CSHS annually inducts new members in October.

Requirements to Join

Must have taken APCSP or AP CSA
Must have a good acedemic standing
Be dedicated and interested in Computer Science

How to Join

In order to be eligible for induction, you must fill out the application, which will be made available in either September or October
See Ms. Norris or any of our officers for help or information about the application process, and any questions about CSHS.
