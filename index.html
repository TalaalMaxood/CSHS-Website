Hello! Welcome to the Deep Run Computer Science Honor Society

Our Purpose:

As stated in the Virginia Computer Science Honor Society Constitution, the Deep Run Computer
Science Honor Society aims to create enthusiasm for computer science and to promote and
recognize academic excellence the Computing Science disciplines in the students of secondary
schools.Through service projects and active tutoring, we hope to better our school and community.


Become a Member...

Every year, at the beginning of the year, students who wish to be in CSHS apply to this organization.
In order to apply, one must have taken AP Computer Science Principles or AP Computer Science A,
and have had a good standing in the class. After applications have been reviewed, those who have
been inducted will recieve a letter inviting them to be inducted into CSHS during our annual induction
ceremony.
